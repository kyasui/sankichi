ABOUT

Yuji Yasui hails from the Nasu region of Tochigi Prefecture, Japan. After moving to Mashiko in the 70’s, he studied ceramics under Tappo Narui.

Since moving to America in the early 90’s, he’s been hand-crafting classic ceramic pieces and firing them in his own Noborigama kiln. For nearly twenty years he’s resided in Maine, refining and perpetuating his art.

His work takes advantage of the natural variations of fire and ash deposited by the path of fire through the kiln.

For all inquiries: info@sankichi.co
